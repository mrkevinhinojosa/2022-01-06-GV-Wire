Prosecution Continues for Valley Men Charged in Capitol Riot

Benjamin Martin, of Madera, faces six federal charges for his role in the Jan. 6 attacks. The charges range from trespassing to obstructing law enforcement. Ricky Willden, of Oakhurst, is charged with eight federal counts, including assault and engaging in violent acts.

Past 10 Years in Fresno Were the Hottest Decade on Record

According to National Weather Service historical records for Fresno, 2012 through 2021 were the hottest years on record. 2014 topped the chart with an annual mean temperature of 69 degrees. By contrast, the lowest annual mean was 60.5 degrees in 1975.

Fresno Kidnapping Claims Cause Social Media Frenzy

In recent weeks, social media posts have claimed that several Fresno area women had gone missing or been abducted from grocery store parking lots and other shopping venues. The Fresno Police Department says the posts are not accurate.
